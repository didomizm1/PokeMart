What is PokeMart

A Poké Mart is a place where the player can buy items for their Pokémon. It has people to talk to who can give hints on some of the items.

What Items are available in PokeMart

Some of the Items that are available are Pokeballs, Full Heal, Antidote,and Revive

Can You order Items Online

Yes, you can order items for your Pokemon online at our website.
